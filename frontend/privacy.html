Overview

We operate services that help communities manage and secure their servers. We prioritize privacy and security by collecting only what is necessary, protecting it with appropriate safeguards, and giving you control over your data.

Information We Collect

Account and Profile

  • Identifiers such as your Discord user ID, username, discriminator, and avatar.
  • Basic profile details you choose to share during authentication.

Server and Configuration

  • Server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and permission configurations necessary to run our bots.
  • Moderation settings, automations, and feature preferences you configure.

Usage and Telemetry

  • Command executions, feature usage, and performance metrics.
  • Log data such as timestamps, IP address, device/browser type, and coarse location derived from IP.

Support and Communications

  • Messages you send to support, feedback submissions, and ticket details.
  • Contact information you provide for responses.

Payment

If you purchase paid features, we process payments via trusted providers. We receive limited billing metadata (e.g., status, plan, last four digits, country) and do not store full card numbers.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate our services, including bot features, dashboards, and integrations.
  • Maintain safety and integrity, including fraud and abuse prevention and incident response.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience through analytics and testing.
  • Communicate with you about updates, changes, and support; you can opt out of non-essential emails.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to enable core site functions and may use optional analytics to understand feature adoption and improve the service. You can control cookies via your browser settings. Where required by law, we will request your consent for non-essential cookies.

Data Sharing

  • Service providers that host infrastructure, analytics, payments, and support tooling under contracts that protect your data.
  • Third‑party integrations you explicitly connect (e.g., messaging platforms); data shared follows your configuration.
  • Legal and safety disclosures where necessary to protect rights, users, or comply with law.
  • Business transfers if we undergo a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice where required.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. When transferring data internationally, we implement safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses and provider due diligence to protect your information.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Configuration data can typically be removed by deleting or disconnecting the bot, and certain logs are rotated or anonymized after a defined period.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, objection, and withdrawal of consent. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity to process requests.

Security

We apply administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your data, including access controls, encrypted transport, least‑privilege principles, and monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we strive to use commercially reasonable safeguards.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age required by platform terms. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided information, contact us to request deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated version with a new “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.

Contact

If you have questions or requests about this policy or your data, contact us at support@agency.dev or via our community channels.
